Biography and History

Karry Wang born on September 21 1999 in Chongqing, China, is a male singer, actor and the leader of Chinese boy band called TFBoys.
Karry began his career when he entered TF entertainment as a trainee in 2010. In 2011, many trainees from TF entertainment have quit and only Karry persevered. Around February 2012, Karry did covers for several Chinese songs including Qiu niao and You Exist In My Song which helped him rise to popularity.
On 6 August 2013, TF entertainment released TFBoys' debut video to introduce the official debut of the three boys who are Karry Wang, Roy Wang and Jackson Yi.
Karry Wang has starred in various movies, such as, The Great Wall and Zhu Xian Yun Zhi . He also starred in a drama named Super Password juvenile in 2016.
